# AI Beauty Business Productivity Assistant App

## Project Overview
AI Beauty Business Productivity Assistant is an AI-powered workplace productivity application designed for beauty professionals, salon owners, skincare consultants, and small beauty businesses.

The application helps automate repetitive workplace tasks such as writing emails, summarizing meetings, scheduling tasks, researching beauty trends, and answering workplace-related questions using Artificial Intelligence.

## Problem Statement
Beauty professionals and small businesses often spend significant time handling repetitive administrative and communication tasks. This reduces productivity and takes time away from clients and business growth.

This project addresses that challenge by using AI to automate common workplace processes and improve efficiency.

## Features

### Smart Email Generator
- Generate professional emails
- Multiple tone options:
  - Formal
  - Friendly
  - Persuasive
- Supports different audiences such as clients and team members

### Meeting Notes Summarizer
- Summarizes lengthy notes
- Extracts:
  - Key points
  - Action items
  - Deadlines
  - Responsibilities

### AI Task Planner
- Creates daily and weekly schedules
- Prioritizes tasks by urgency and importance
- Improves time management

### AI Research Assistant
- Researches skincare, haircare, and beauty industry trends
- Provides summaries and recommendations

### AI Chatbot Assistant
- Interactive workplace assistant
- Answers questions related to beauty business productivity

## Prompt Engineering Strategy
Structured prompts were used to improve AI output quality by including:

- Context
- User goals
- Tone selection
- Audience type
- Task requirements

## Responsible AI
This application follows responsible AI practices:

- AI outputs may contain inaccuracies
- Users should verify generated content
- Human review is recommended before making business decisions
- AI recommendations do not replace professional or medical advice
